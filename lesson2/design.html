Visual Hierarchy

Amazon

amazon.com

Visual hierarchy allows visitors to figure out the most important elements of a web page without having to read through the page. Amazon uses this technique in their website. The "Add to Cart" and "Buy Now" buttons are the main call to actions of a product page, hence the emphases in color, contrast and size of the buttons compared to the other elements on the page

Contrast

Lush

Lush.com

A basic principle of contrast is that the higher the contrast between two items, the easier it is to visually separate them. It’s the delicious colouring of the toiletries, contrasted with large black and white typography and a black header that makes this such an enjoyable site to browse.

White Space

Quip

quip.com

Empty space is not always wasted space. In fact, when it comes to web design, it's a best practice to give your content a little breathing room. On its website, Quip uses active whitespace in the most straightforward sense, but with purpose. By its name, Quip, one would not automatically assume the company sells toothbrushes. Placing the toothbrush front and center surrounded by whitespace guides the user’s eye directly to the subject and without question, it’s clear what the company does.
